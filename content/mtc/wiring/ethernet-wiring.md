---
title: "Ethernet Wiring"
weight: 3
---

The MTC16 uses 10/100Base-T Ethernet communications. It only requires 4 wires (2 pairs) to operate.

| Signal | MTC Pin | RJ45 Pin | Colour       |
|--------|---------|----------|--------------|
| Rx+    | C20     | 3        | Orange/White |
| Rx-    | C21     | 6        | Orange       |
| Tx+    | C22     | 1        | Green/White  |
| Tx-    | C23     | 2        | Green        |

> No special ethernet configuration is required. MectriCal will detect the device using an IPv6 Link Local Address.
